postcard travels

  • Uses:

    - for pictures of new places you've gone to both in real life and in fictional experiences such as video games.

    - (Images taken on a phone in landscape mode or images taken in a photo mode in a video game are already in the ideal aspect-ratio)

    - Ideal for: 16 by 9 images

    - Exciting

  • Features:

    - custom background
